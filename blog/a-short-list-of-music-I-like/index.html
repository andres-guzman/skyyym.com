A short list of music I like

January 31, 2019

I should have warned my blog was going to cover mundane topics such as what music I like. I think of it as a way to relieve any possible stress I may have. Be that as it may, this is what's on my playlist for anyone who might be interested.

２８１４ \ A Perfect Cirle \ Alarm Will Sound \ Alice in Chains \ Bisk \ Boards of Canada \ Broadcast \ Chevron \ Crosses \ Customer Service \ Cyclo \ Daniel Johnston \ Dakrypyramid \ Datach'i \ Deftones \ Earth Divided By Zero \ Dumb Type \ Filter \ Flying Testicle \ Ghost \ Ghoulspoon \ Glassjaw \ Gravenhurst \ HKE \ Iacon \ Interpol \ Koji Asano \ Lindemann \ Lykke Li \ Mandalay \ Merzbow \ My Bloody Valentine \ N.E.R.D \ Nada Surf \ Nine Inch Nails \ Nirvana \ Paul Banks \ Peaches \ Pearl Jam \ Plaid \ Portishead \ Rammstein \ Ryoji Ikeda \ Scanner \ Seefeel \ Sepultura \ Sevendust \ Slipknot \ Squarepusher \ Stars of the Lid \ Stereolab \ The Dead Texan \ Venetian Snares \ Wipers \ Yeah Yeah Yeahs \ Yo La Tengo \ μ-Ziq

There are a handful of musicians I hope they come up with some new material some time soon: Aphex Twin, Boards of Canada, A perfect circle, Datach'i, Venetian Snares and some others. I would buy their music with outh much thought. These artists are still active. Of course I would love the Wipers would release some new material but they're long inactive as a band.
